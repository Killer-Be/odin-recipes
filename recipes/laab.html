How to make Laotian Laab

How to make Lao style Chicken Laab gai aka Larb

This is the recipe my Laotian friend Cindy taught me. It's full of many elements of umami, tangy, and bright fresh ingredients. This is the full recipe but really you can omit many steps of this to make it in about 10 minutes (if you have premade rice) for a quick delicious dinner or you can take more prep time and it will have more layers of flavor but take somewhere like 30-45 minutes.

For any folks out there who may be allergic to ingredients such as soy or fish sauce, feel free to substitute out as necessary as long as you replace with an equivalent amount of a similar flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb ground/minced chicken breast
  • 2 fresh limes
  • chinese long bean or similar
  • 2 tsp ground roasted rice
  • 2 tbsp soy or tamari sauce
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce (or to taste)
  • 2 shallots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 fresh birds eye chili
  • 1/2 tsp msg powder
  • handful of fresh mint leaves rougly chopped
  • 1/2 head of napa cabbage leaves washed

Preparation

    • Cook chicken on high temp with wok, don't overcook
    • wash and cut beans to bit size pieces
    • mix liquid ingredients
    • mix beans in to chicken when still cooking
    • add liquid ingredients,and and half of rice
